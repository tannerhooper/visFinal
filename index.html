Results

One of the main purposes in creating this visualization was to determine if pell grants or federal loans helped graduation rates increase throughout the country. Determining if one was greater than the other would give greater incentive for funding to be increased. As we used our visualization and explored our data we found that federal loans had the greatest positive impact in the nation.

Our visualization not only explores which states have the best graduation rates, but also which colleges have the best graduation rates in the state or nation during a selected year. This comprehensive filtering of data can be used by government officials or academic board members when deciding how to help their students. This visualization can also be used for students on deciding which college will help them graduate.
